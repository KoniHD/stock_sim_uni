Method attempts to buy as many Stocks for each stock risk as possible with the given total_funds.

Parameters
total_fundsTotal available funds across all stock risk levels. total_funds will also be spent (i.e. the variable decreases).
grouped_stocksPredetermined container of StockGroups.
risk_percentageArray specifying how much [0] low risk stocks, [1] mid risk stocks, and [2] high risk stocks (in decimal) to buy.
Returns
The Stock portfolio.

The method optimizes the Stock purchase such that first funds for each risk level are determined. The resulting fraction is then divided by the amount of Stocks to create a budget for that Stock. Then the biggest possible amount of Stocks is bought with that budget. Finally using the left-over budget it is attemted to buy the cheapest Stock in the stock risk category.
