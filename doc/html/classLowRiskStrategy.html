Composes a high-risk portfolio of Stocks.

Parameters
total_fundsThe funds available for purchasing Stocks.
stockMarketThe Stock Market to pick Stocks from.
Returns
The Stock portfolio.

This strategy picks 50% low-risk stocks, 30% mid-risk stocks and 20% high-risk stocks. The amount of Stocks per risk level then depends on the total_funds. Per risk category, the value is distributed equally among the Stocks. By this, the number of Stocks in the portoflio is derived. The method also decreases the total_funds passed to it, simulating a real purchase.
